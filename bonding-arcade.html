Tutor notes

This works best when the student explains the link between microscopic structure and macroscopic property. If they get the answer quickly, ask for a contrast case: “What would change if this were simple molecular instead?”

Useful prompts

  • What particles are present?
  • What is the strongest attraction being overcome?
  • Would this conduct as a solid, or only when molten?
  • Is the key feature the bond itself or the overall structure?

How the score works

  • 1 point for the correct structure type.
  • 1 point for the matching property statement.
  • 1 bonus point for getting both right in the same round.
